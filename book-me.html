Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Cameo take?
Standard delivery is 7 days. Rush delivery (24 hours) is available for an additional $50.
Can you do any character?
I can do any character I've voiced (Harold, Gelman, Rhino, etc.) plus original voices if you have something specific in mind.
How are autographs shipped?
All autographs ship in rigid mailers via USPS First Class. International shipping available for +$15.
Can I get a refund?
Cameos: Full refund if I don't deliver on time. Autographs: Refund or replacement if damaged in shipping.
Do you attend conventions?
Yes! I do 4-6 conventions per year. Follow me on Instagram for announcements, or reach out directly for bookings.
Can you sign items I already own?
Yes! Email me at justinfilm@gmail.com to discuss shipping your item to me for signing. Additional fees apply.
