About

Three Decades of Iconic Performances

Justin Shenkarow has spent over three decades building a versatile career across voice overs, on-camera acting, and production, from defining '90s animation to Emmy-winning drama, to pioneering sports entertainment for young audiences.

As a young actor, Justin became the voice of a generation, bringing to life Harold Berman on Nickelodeon's Hey Arnold!, alongside memorable roles in Recess, Lloyd in Space, and Life with Louie. He even voiced Izzy, the official (and slightly chaotic) mascot of the 1996 Atlanta Olympics.

On screen, he earned Emmy and Golden Globe awards as part of the Best Ensemble Cast on CBS's groundbreaking Picket Fences as Matthew Brock, and starred as the conspiracy-hunting Simon Holmes on NBC's cult classic Eerie, Indiana. He transitioned into adult acting across film and television, including Z Nation and many others.

In the voice booth, Justin's range spans everything from Avatar, Good Will Hunting, and Silver Linings Playbook to Ice Age, Bee Movie, and Shrek the Third. Currently, he voices the incredible Rhino on Marvel's Spidey and His Amazing Friends.

A Stanford graduate with an MBA, and Chinese Government Scholar who studied in Shanghai, Justin has always mixed creativity with strategy. He served on the SAG-AFTRA Board and as National Chairman of the Young Performers Committee, advocating for every child actor in the industry.

As a producer and creator, Justin develops and options projects spanning true crime and stories inspired by real events. He's co-founder of Tzero Studios and co-creator of Bronx Buds, the Emmy Award-winning animated series produced with New York Yankees Entertainment where he continues to create amazing content for the next generation of kids.
