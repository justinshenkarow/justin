About

Three Decades of Iconic Performances

Justin Shenkarow has built a career spanning decades in Hollywood, earning recognition across live-action television, feature film, voice acting, and production. Best known for voicing Harold Berman on Nickelodeon's beloved Hey Arnold!, Justin has left his mark on some of the most iconic entertainment of the past three decades.

On screen, he starred as Matthew Brock on CBS's Emmy-winning Picket Fences and as Simon Holmes on NBC's cult classic Eerie, Indiana. His film credits include Avatar, Good Will Hunting, and Silver Linings Playbook.

In the voice booth, Justin has contributed to Ice Age, Bee Movie, and Shrek the Third, and currently voices Rhino on Marvel's Spidey and His Amazing Friends. A Stanford University graduate, SAG-AFTRA board member, and co-founder of TZero Studios, Justin continues to push creative boundaries.
